Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know to start your teletherapy journey with confidence.

Is teletherapy as effective as in-person sessions?

Yes! Our licensed professionals use structured, personalized plans that deliver measurable results — all from the comfort of your home.

Who will I be matched with?

Our clinical team carefully reviews your intake form and assigns you the most suitable specialist — or a multidisciplinary team for holistic support.

How do I know the professionals are qualified?

All therapists are licensed, verified, and experienced in their respective fields — from speech therapy to mental health counseling.

Is my information safe and confidential?

Absolutely. All your data and session details are securely encrypted and handled with the highest confidentiality standards.

Can I schedule sessions around my availability?

Yes! Flexible scheduling ensures you can access therapy at times that work for you — including evenings and weekends.

What if I need multiple types of therapy?

We provide holistic care by coordinating multiple specialists if needed, so your therapy plan is fully personalized.

How do payments work?

Transparent pricing with no hidden fees. You can pay per session or choose a subscription package that best fits your needs.

What if I need to reschedule?

You can easily reschedule a session through our platform — your continuity of care is our priority.
